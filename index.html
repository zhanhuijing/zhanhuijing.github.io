Short Bio

My name is Zhan Huijing, currently a senior research scientist in Machine Intellection (MI) Department of Institute for Infocomm Research (I2R). My current research interests are personalized recommendation, AI in education, fashion image analysis and 3D object detection.

I received the PhD degree from Nanyang Technological University (NTU), advised by Prof. Alex Kot. I obtained Bachelor degree in Advanced Class of Electrical Engineering department in Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2012.
